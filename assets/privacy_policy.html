Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Lifeforge: Arcanist's Ledger ("the App") is a life-counter utility for tabletop trading card games. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App does and does not collect.

1. No Personal Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. Player names you enter are stored only in your device's local memory for the duration of a game session and are never sent anywhere.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking tools of any kind.

3. External Network Requests

The App optionally contacts the Scryfall API (scryfall.com) to search for card artwork and names when you look up a commander. This is a read-only request containing only a card search query. No personal data is sent. Scryfall's own privacy policy applies to their service. All API requests are optional; the App functions fully offline using a built-in card list.

4. No Third-Party SDKs That Collect Data

The App is built with Expo / React Native and uses only the following libraries:

  • expo (open-source, no data collection)
  • expo-linear-gradient (rendering only)
  • expo-status-bar (UI only)
  • lucide-react-native (icons only)
  • react-native-svg (rendering only)

None of these libraries collect user data.

5. Device Permissions

The App requests no device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive device capability.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected from any user, this App is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, an updated version will be published here with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer at the email address listed on the Google Play Store listing.
